Introduction Professional Artifacts

These are some examples of my work from Boise State University and my time studying the GIMM major.

Third Circle Cafe

Third Circle Cafe is a game that revolves around a hellish man making cakes for hellish creatures an an AR game space. My key contribution to the game was creating the physical attributes that you can to get into the AR minigames.

Infinite Impermanence

Infinite Impermanence is a game that revolves around three main characters trying to solve a murder mystery and a time loop phenomenon. This game was created through Unity and is a 2D platformer mixed with a comic book. I was tasked within my team to create the backgrounds, platforms, and UI.

Fantasy Unreal Environment

This project was created in Unreal Engine. It was largely inspired by The Legend of Zelda Korok Forest, DnD, and Alice in Wonderland. Much like in Alice in Wonderland, you are made to be a small human walking through a vast fantasy world and through extremely tall grasses until you finally reach a clearing wwhere you see a beautiful pond and the Frog King, voiced by me.
